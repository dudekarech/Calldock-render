Demo Content

This is a demonstration page showing how the CallDocker widget integrates seamlessly with any website. The widget appears as a floating button that users can click to initiate calls with your support team.

The widget is fully customizable through the admin dashboard, allowing you to:

  • Choose from multiple themes (default, dark, brand colors)
  • Select different sizes (compact, standard, large)
  • Position the widget in any corner of the screen
  • Add your company logo and branding
  • Customize the call flow and routing

The widget automatically handles call routing, agent assignment, and provides a smooth user experience for your customers seeking support.
